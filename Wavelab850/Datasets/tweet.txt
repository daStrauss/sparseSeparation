tweet.asc -- Digital signal of a bird singing

Access
    Tweet = ReadSignal('Tweet');
  
Size
    8,192 by 1

Sampling Rate
    22 kHz
    
Source
    Doug Jones, University of Illinois
    digitized from D.J. Borror, Common Bird Songs (Cassette),
    Dover Publications, Inc., New York, 1984.
  
Description
    In MATLAB, the command sound(Tweet,22000) will play
    this sound back at the right pitch.
